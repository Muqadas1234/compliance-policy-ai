POLICY 1: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
================================================================================
Policy ID: FIN-001
Category: Financial Compliance

All employee expense reimbursements must adhere to the following limits:
- Single meal expense: Maximum $50 USD
- Daily meal allowance: Maximum $150 USD
- Hotel accommodation: Maximum $300 per night
- Domestic flight: Economy class only, maximum $800
- International flight: Business class allowed only for flights over 6 hours
- Ground transportation: Maximum $100 per day
- Total monthly reimbursement: Maximum $5,000 USD per employee

All expenses must include:
- Original itemized receipts
- Business justification
- Manager pre-approval for expenses over $500
- Finance Director approval for expenses over $2,000

Violation: Expenses exceeding limits without approval will be rejected and may result in disciplinary action.

POLICY 2: DATA PRIVACY AND PROTECTION
================================================================================
Policy ID: SEC-002
Category: Information Security

All handling of customer and employee personal data must comply with:
- No storage of personal data on personal devices
- No sharing of customer data outside approved systems
- All personal data must be encrypted at rest and in transit
- Access to personal data requires role-based authorization
- Customer data retention: Maximum 7 years after relationship ends
- Employee data retention: Maximum 10 years after employment ends
- Data deletion requests must be processed within 30 days
- No transfer of personal data to third parties without written consent
- All data breaches must be reported to Security team within 1 hour

Prohibited Actions:
- Emailing unencrypted customer data
- Storing passwords in plain text
- Accessing data without business need
- Using personal email for company data

Violation: Immediate suspension and potential termination. Legal action may be pursued.

POLICY 3: VENDOR AND SUPPLIER APPROVAL
================================================================================
Policy ID: PROC-003
Category: Procurement Compliance

All vendor engagements must follow these requirements:

Vendor Selection:
- Contracts under $10,000: Department manager approval
- Contracts $10,000 - $50,000: Director approval + Procurement review
- Contracts over $50,000: Executive approval + Legal review + Compliance check
- All vendors must pass background check and compliance screening

Required Documentation:
- W9 or W8 tax forms
- Certificate of Insurance
- Signed vendor agreement
- Conflict of interest disclosure
- Data security assessment (if handling company data)

Payment Terms:
- Net 30 standard payment terms
- Early payment requires CFO approval
- No cash payments over $500
- All payments require invoice with PO number

Prohibited Vendors:
- Vendors on government sanction lists
- Vendors with known compliance violations
- Vendors refusing background checks
- Vendors with conflicts of interest

Violation: Contract void, payment withheld, potential legal action.

POLICY 4: FINANCIAL TRANSACTION LIMITS
================================================================================
Policy ID: FIN-004
Category: Financial Controls

Authorization limits for financial transactions:

Wire Transfers:
- Under $25,000: Finance Manager approval
- $25,000 - $100,000: CFO approval
- Over $100,000: CFO + CEO dual approval

Check Payments:
- Under $5,000: Accounts Payable team
- $5,000 - $25,000: Finance Manager approval
- Over $25,000: CFO approval

Credit Card Limits:
- Employee cards: $5,000 per month
- Manager cards: $15,000 per month
- Director cards: $50,000 per month
- All transactions must be reconciled within 7 days

International Transactions:
- Require additional compliance review
- Must verify recipient is not on sanctions list
- Currency conversion must be documented
- Payments to high-risk countries require Legal approval

Violation: Transaction blocked, card suspended, disciplinary action.

POLICY 5: EMPLOYEE CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================
Policy ID: HR-005
Category: Human Resources Compliance

All employees must adhere to professional conduct standards:

Prohibited Conduct:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Conflicts of interest without disclosure
- Accepting gifts over $100 from vendors or clients
- Working for competitors without approval
- Disclosure of confidential company information
- Use of company resources for personal gain
- Insider trading or securities violations

Required Actions:
- Report conflicts of interest within 5 days
- Complete annual compliance training
- Report ethical violations to Ethics Hotline
- Disclose outside employment or board positions
- Sign annual code of conduct acknowledgment

Gift Policy:
- Gifts under $50: Acceptable
- Gifts $50-$100: Manager disclosure required
- Gifts over $100: Must be declined or donated to charity
- Cash gifts: Never acceptable

Violation: Disciplinary action up to and including termination. Criminal conduct will be reported to authorities.

POLICY 6: DOCUMENT RETENTION AND DESTRUCTION
================================================================================
Policy ID: LEGAL-006
Category: Legal Compliance

Document retention requirements by category:

Financial Records:
- Tax returns: 7 years
- Invoices and receipts: 7 years
- Payroll records: 7 years
- Audit reports: Permanent
- Bank statements: 7 years

Employee Records:
- Personnel files: 7 years after termination
- Benefits records: 7 years after separation
- I-9 forms: 3 years after hire or 1 year after termination (whichever is later)
- Performance reviews: 5 years
- Termination records: 7 years

Contracts and Agreements:
- Active contracts: Permanent until 7 years after expiration
- Vendor agreements: 7 years after termination
- Customer contracts: 7 years after end of relationship
- Real estate leases: Permanent

Prohibited Actions:
- Destroying documents under legal hold
- Personal retention of company documents
- Storing documents beyond retention period without Legal approval
- Improper disposal of confidential documents (must be shredded)

Violation: Legal consequences, potential obstruction charges, termination.

POLICY 7: ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AML)
================================================================================
Policy ID: COMP-007
Category: Regulatory Compliance

All financial transactions must be monitored for money laundering risks:

Red Flags Requiring Investigation:
- Cash transactions over $10,000
- Multiple transactions just below reporting thresholds
- Transactions with high-risk countries
- Customer reluctance to provide information
- Unusual transaction patterns
- Transactions inconsistent with business profile

Required Actions:
- Customer due diligence for all new accounts
- Enhanced due diligence for high-risk customers
- Transaction monitoring for suspicious activity
- File Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) within 30 days of detection
- Maintain transaction records for 5 years
- Annual AML training for all relevant employees

Prohibited Transactions:
- Transactions with sanctioned entities
- Structuring to avoid reporting requirements
- Transactions lacking business purpose
- Anonymous transactions over $3,000

Violation: Regulatory fines, criminal prosecution, license revocation.

POLICY 8: INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ACCEPTABLE USE
================================================================================
Policy ID: IT-008
Category: Technology Compliance

Company technology resources must be used appropriately:

Acceptable Use:
- Business purposes only
- Minimal personal use permitted during breaks
- Access only to authorized systems and data
- Use of approved software only
- Remote access via VPN only

Prohibited Use:
- Downloading unauthorized software
- Visiting malicious or inappropriate websites
- Sharing login credentials
- Using personal cloud storage for company data
- Connecting unauthorized devices to network
- Bypassing security controls
- Cryptocurrency mining on company systems

Security Requirements:
- Strong passwords (12+ characters, changed every 90 days)
- Multi-factor authentication for all critical systems
- Lock screen when away from desk
- Report security incidents within 1 hour
- No sharing of credentials
- Encrypt all portable devices

Software Licensing:
- Use only licensed software
- No installation of unlicensed software
- Annual software audit compliance required
- Report unlicensed software immediately

Violation: System access revoked, disciplinary action, potential criminal charges for severe violations.

POLICY 9: CONFLICT OF INTEREST
================================================================================
Policy ID: ETH-009
Category: Ethics and Compliance

Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with company interests:

Disclosure Requirements:
- Financial interests in competitors, customers, or vendors
- Family members employed by business partners
- Outside employment or consulting
- Board positions at other organizations
- Personal business relationships with company stakeholders

Prohibited Conflicts:
- Hiring family members without disclosure and approval
- Awarding contracts to companies where employee has financial interest
- Accepting personal benefits from vendors
- Competing with company while employed
- Using company information for personal gain

Review Process:
- Submit conflict disclosure form within 5 business days
- Annual conflict of interest certification required
- Ethics Committee reviews all disclosures
- Approval or mitigation plan provided within 15 days
- Recusal from decisions involving conflict

Violation: Termination, disgorgement of profits, potential legal action.

POLICY 10: WORKPLACE SAFETY AND HEALTH
================================================================================
Policy ID: SAFE-010
Category: Health and Safety Compliance

All employees must maintain safe working environment:

Safety Requirements:
- Report all accidents and injuries within 24 hours
- Participate in emergency drills
- Use personal protective equipment (PPE) when required
- Follow ergonomic guidelines for workstation setup
- Report safety hazards immediately
- Complete annual safety training

Prohibited Actions:
- Working under influence of drugs or alcohol
- Bypassing safety equipment or procedures
- Blocking emergency exits
- Ignoring safety warnings
- Operating equipment without training
- Creating unsafe conditions

Incident Reporting:
- Minor injuries: Report to supervisor immediately
- Major injuries: Call emergency services + notify Safety Officer
- Near misses: Submit safety report within 24 hours
- Property damage: Report to Facilities within 4 hours
- All incidents documented in safety management system

Drug and Alcohol Policy:
- Zero tolerance for impairment at work
- Random testing for safety-sensitive positions
- Post-accident testing required
- Refused testing treated as positive result

Violation: Disciplinary action, potential termination, workers compensation denial for policy violations.
